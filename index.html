✦ Our Story

Born from a Love for India

We are WanderIndia

Made by a group of 5 individuals with one shared belief: India is one of the world's greatest travel destinations, and it deserves a platform built with the same passion as the country itself.

From the snow-capped Himalayas to the lush backwaters of Kerala, from the pink-walled lanes of Jaipur to the neon chaos of Mumbai at midnight — WanderIndia exists to help every traveller find their own version of this magnificent country.

We help you find a city, know more about the places you can visit along with the hotels in the area. We're your travel companion, your cultural guide, your local friend who knows the best chai stall near every monument.

✦ What Drives Us
